6. ISO 9001 clauses covered

Clause Requirement Where it lives in the redesign
8.7.1Identify & control non-conforming output to prevent unintended useContainment Action field + status workflow keeps job out of next stage until disposition
8.7.1 a–dDisposition options (correct, segregate, return, etc.)Disposition field — Reworked / Scrapped / Use-as-is / Returned / Other
8.7.2Documented information on actions taken & authority approvingTwo-name sign-off trail (PE + QHSE), stored in CPARLog SP list, exportable to PDF/xlsx
10.2.1 aReact to nonconformity, contain, deal with consequencesQHSE Triage stage — Jonas reviews every CPAR within SLA
10.2.1 bEvaluate need for action to eliminate causeInvestigation stage — assigned to a named PE (Mark or Gareth)
10.2.1 cImplement action neededCorrective Action field — including ECR raise for spec issues
10.2.1 dUpdate risks and opportunities if necessaryLinked ECR (Engineering Change Request) ref captured back to CPAR
10.2.2Retain documented information as evidenceCPARLog SharePoint list (already exists); PDF print-out attached to physical job folder

Not covered yet (deferred to Approach 3 / phase 2): §10.2.1 e — review effectiveness of corrective action after N days. Could add a "30-day re-check" prompt later.
